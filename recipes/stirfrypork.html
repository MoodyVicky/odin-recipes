Stir Fry Pork

Rich and spicy stir fry pork with minimal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Doubanjiang
  • Soy Sauce
  • Mirin
  • Ginger
  • Small Onion
  • Thin Sliced Pork Belly
  • *Cooking oil and hot water*

How to prepare

  1. Slice onion and cut pork belly into one inch pieces.
  2. Combine ginger and liquid ingredients in small bowl and add one teaspoon of hot water.
  3. Heat pan to medium and add oil.
  4. Once oil is heated, add onions. Stir fry for one minute
  5. Add pork and cook about 90%.
  6. Lower heat and add sauce.
  7. Cook through then garnish and serve.
