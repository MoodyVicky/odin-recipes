Egg Sandwich

Delicious and savory egg sandwich, just like mom makes

Ingredients

  • 3 Eggs
  • Fish Sauce
  • MSG
  • Swiss Cheese
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Butter
  • *Cooking oil*

How to prepare

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk, combining the egg whites and yolk well.
  2. Add fish sauce and MSG.
  3. Heat pan to medium and add oil.
  4. Once oil is heated, add egg mixture.
  5. Cook through and add cheese on top. Cover with lid to melt cheese.
  6. Butter and toast bread.
  7. Place egg and cheese combination in between the toasted bread. Enjoy
